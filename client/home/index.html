Group 2's Website

Welcome to Group 2's website.

The problem that we are trying to better understand and potentially solve is the seeking of mental health services for freshmen. The UMD Health Center website discusses how the transition to college can be stressful. The Health Center also offers several links and phone numbers for freshmen to use for mental health services. Our project will be to understand how to better provide mental health services to freshmen. Once we better understand the problem, we will implement a UX solution that we, with the help of the interviews, hope will solve the problem.
